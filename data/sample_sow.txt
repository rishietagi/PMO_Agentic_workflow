Statement of Work — CRM Cloud Migration Program

Background:
Acme Industrial currently runs a heavily customized on-premise CRM that is
end-of-life. Leadership has approved migrating to a cloud SaaS CRM to reduce
maintenance cost, improve uptime, and enable a mobile field-sales workforce.

Objectives:
- Migrate all customer, contact, and opportunity data to the new SaaS CRM.
- Cut over 400 users across sales, service, and marketing in 3 regions.
- Integrate the CRM with the existing ERP and marketing automation tools.
- Decommission the legacy CRM and its supporting infrastructure.

Scope:
In scope: data migration, integration build, user provisioning, training,
and hypercare. Out of scope: replacing the ERP, net-new analytics warehouse.

Key deliverables:
- Migrated and validated production CRM tenant.
- Two bidirectional integrations (ERP, marketing automation).
- Role-based training materials and 4 weeks of post-go-live hypercare.

Constraints:
- Fixed budget of $1.2M and a 9-month timeline (must finish before fiscal
  year end).
- No data loss; regulated customer data must remain in-region.
- Field sales cannot be offline for more than one business day at cutover.

Assumptions:
- SaaS vendor and licenses are already selected and procured.
- Business SMEs are available 20% of their time for the project duration.

Stakeholders:
VP Sales (sponsor), CRM Product Owner, IT Security, Regional Sales Directors,
Customer Service Lead, Data Privacy Officer.

Timeline: 9 months. Budget: $1.2M.
